How it decodes. Native BarcodeDetector runs first where the browser has it — detection happens in platform code, on the device. Where it's missing, a bundled WASM decoder takes over with no network call either way. If a straight read finds nothing, the image is re-tried at 90°, 180° and 270°, then at 2× scale for small or low-contrast codes.
